Supporting Mitsui's Strategic Investment in ACI Motors: A Cross-Border Equity Transaction

USD 22.75M · 18.5% Equity Stake · ACI Motors Limited

Project Overview

One of the most significant projects in my corporate finance career was supporting Mitsui & Co., Ltd.'s strategic investment in ACI Motors Limited, where Mitsui acquired an 18.5% equity stake valued at approximately USD 22.75 million.

Over nearly ten months, I worked as part of ACI's Corporate Finance team, contributing to the execution of this cross-border equity transaction. The project provided valuable exposure to business valuation, regulatory compliance, transaction execution, and stakeholder management.

Source: The Daily Star — read the news coverage

My Contribution

I supported the transaction by coordinating financial analyses, regulatory documentation, and communication between internal teams and external stakeholders.

My key responsibilities included:

  • Leading the valuation exercise from ACI's side in collaboration with the appointed merchant bank
  • Developing valuation models in accordance with Bangladesh Bank guidelines using the Asset-Based Approach, Discounted Cash Flow (DCF), and Relative Valuation methodologies
  • Preparing and reviewing transaction-related documents for regulatory approvals and share transfer
  • Coordinating with lending banks to obtain No Objection Certificates (NOCs), including preparing supporting documents and FAQs to address lenders' queries
  • Working closely with legal teams, advisors, and internal stakeholders to ensure timely completion of transaction milestones

Key Challenges

This transaction required balancing analytical work with extensive stakeholder coordination. Some of the key challenges included:

  • Supporting a cross-border strategic investment involving multiple stakeholders
  • Developing valuation analyses using multiple methodologies to support transaction pricing
  • Managing regulatory documentation and lender approvals within tight timelines
First-hand exposure to the execution of a strategic equity investment, from valuation through to close.

Professional Growth

This project significantly strengthened both my technical and execution capabilities. Beyond applying valuation methodologies in a live transaction, I gained practical experience in coordinating complex corporate finance projects involving advisors, regulators, legal teams, and financial institutions.

The experience reinforced my expertise in business valuation, transaction execution, and stakeholder management while providing first-hand exposure to the execution of a strategic equity investment.
