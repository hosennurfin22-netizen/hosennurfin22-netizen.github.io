ACI Sukuk Programme: My First Large-Scale Capital Market Transaction

BDT 2,400Cr Programme (BDT 600Cr First Tranche) · ACI Group

Project Overview

One of the most rewarding assignments in my career was contributing to ACI Limited's inaugural Sukuk programme — a landmark initiative designed to diversify the company's long-term funding sources through the capital market.

The programme envisioned raising BDT 2,400 crore, with a first tranche of BDT 600 crore. Riverstone Capital Limited served as the Issue Manager and Transaction Advisor, while DBH Finance PLC was appointed as the Trustee.

Although the transaction was ultimately not completed following changes in the regulatory environment after July 2024, working on the project for approximately 18 months provided me with comprehensive, hands-on exposure to the execution of a large-scale capital market transaction from the issuer's perspective.

Source: The Business Standard — read the news coverage

My Role

As a Corporate Financial Analyst, I was entrusted with coordinating numerous workstreams on behalf of ACI throughout the preparation phase of the Sukuk issuance. The project required close collaboration across multiple internal departments and external stakeholders, ensuring that regulatory submissions, financial information, and transaction documentation were prepared accurately and delivered within demanding timelines.

My key responsibilities included:

  • Coordinating transaction activities involving ten originating entities across the ACI Group
  • Serving as a key liaison between ACI and external stakeholders, including the Issue Manager (Riverstone Capital), Trustee (DBH Finance PLC), the Shariah Advisory Board, the BSEC, auditors, lenders, and legal advisors
  • Preparing historical and projected financial information required to support the issuance and regulatory submissions
  • Supporting the preparation and review of the Information Memorandum and other transaction documents developed by the Issue Manager
  • Drafting regulatory correspondence and coordinating responses to BSEC observations, information requests, and submission deficiencies
  • Preparing supporting information and documentation required for the Shariah pronouncement process
  • Coordinating asset-related schedules, statutory documents, auditor certifications, director CIB reports, lender information, and other regulatory requirements
  • Preparing draft No Objection Certificate (NOC) requests and coordinating with lending banks to facilitate regulatory compliance
  • Working closely with both ACI's legal team and external legal counsel to support transaction documentation and ensure compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements
  • Managing multiple workstreams simultaneously while ensuring timely completion of deliverables across various stakeholders

Financial Analysis & Strategic Support

Beyond transaction coordination, I contributed analytical work that supported both management decision-making and the overall structuring of the issuance:

  • Developing projected financial statements for all ten originating entities to support the Sukuk structure
  • Performing a comparative cost-benefit analysis between Sukuk financing and conventional bank borrowing, enabling management to evaluate alternative funding strategies
  • Preparing executive presentation materials summarizing the Sukuk structure, financing terms, and key transaction considerations
  • Presenting the proposed Sukuk framework and term sheet before the CFO, directors, and senior management, facilitating strategic discussions around the proposed financing

These responsibilities strengthened my ability to translate complex financial information into practical insights for executive decision-making.

Challenges

The project involved coordinating a highly complex transaction with numerous stakeholders, each operating under different regulatory, legal, and commercial requirements:

  • Coordinating information and documentation across ten originating entities
  • Managing interactions among finance, legal, advisory, regulatory, and business teams
  • Responding promptly to regulatory observations while maintaining documentation quality
  • Ensuring multiple workstreams progressed simultaneously under strict submission deadlines
  • Navigating the unique documentation and compliance requirements associated with a Shariah-compliant capital market instrument

Successfully managing these challenges required strong organizational skills, stakeholder management, attention to detail, and the ability to perform effectively under pressure.

The experience provided invaluable exposure to the full lifecycle of a public capital market transaction.

Professional Growth

This project became one of the most significant learning experiences of my career. Through active involvement in the transaction, I developed practical experience in capital market fundraising, Sukuk and Islamic finance structures, financial modelling and forecasting, corporate finance and investment analysis, transaction documentation, regulatory engagement with the BSEC, executive presentation and communication, cross-functional stakeholder management, large-scale project coordination, and managing complex transactions under tight regulatory timelines.

While the issuance was ultimately not completed, the experience provided invaluable exposure to the full lifecycle of a public capital market transaction — from financial analysis and documentation to regulatory engagement, stakeholder coordination, and executive communication.
